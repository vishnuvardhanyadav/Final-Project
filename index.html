    Numerical Ability

    Numerical ability refers to a person's skill in working with numbers and mathematical concepts. It is essential in fields that require quantitative analysis, such as finance, engineering, and data science. Key areas include:
  • Basic Arithmetic
  • Algibra
  • Data interpretation
  • Problem-solving

    Reasoning Ability

    Reasoning Ability refers to a person's natural ability to learn or perform in a particular area. It is often assessed to predict how well someone might perform in a specific job or educational program. Aptitude tests can measure a variety of skills, including:
  • Basic Arithmetic
  • Algibra
  • Data interpretation
  • Problem-solving

    Verbal Ability

    Verbal ability involves skills related to understanding, analyzing, and using language. It is a crucial part of communication and often assessed in various exams. Key components include:
  • Vocabulary
  • Grammer
  • Reading comprehension
